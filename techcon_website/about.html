Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for innovators and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest conferences in the tech world.

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and connect tech professionals from around the globe, while promoting innovation, diversity, and inclusion in the industry.
